Features

Everything you need to focus

Built for people who know they're easily distracted and want to do something about it.

🧠
Smart detection

Automatically detects distracting sites using a built-in category engine covering social media, news, gaming, entertainment, NSFW, and more. Optional AI layer for edge cases.

🎯
Goal-aware

Set your current goals in plain English. Every intervention reminds you what you're working toward, not just that you got caught.

🔥
Five intervention modes

From a gentle motivational nudge to a full chaos-mode ad wall. Pick how hard you want to be stopped.

🚫

⚡

How it works

Three steps, then it runs itself

Set it up once and Lock In watches every tab you open from then on.

1
Set your goals

Open Settings and write down what you're working toward — your CS project, exam prep, a deadline. The more specific, the better the motivation when you need it.

2
Choose how hard to be stopped

Pick your intervention style — a gentle popup, a reflection prompt, a timed countdown, a full-page redirect, or Chaos Mode, which fills the screen with fake ads until you close them.

3
Lock in and get to work

Lock In watches every navigation in the background. The moment you drift toward a distracting site, it intervenes with your goals front and center.

Intervention modes

Choose your difficulty

Pick the level of accountability that actually works for you.

💬
Motivational Popup

A gentle nudge in the corner. Dismisses after 12 seconds.

🤔
Reflection Prompt

"Was this intentional?" Choose to go back or continue consciously.

⏱️
Countdown Timer

Full-screen overlay. You have to wait before you can proceed.

🚫
Full Redirect

Replaces the tab entirely. Proceed button unlocks after 5 seconds.

🔥
Chaos Mode

Fills the screen with obnoxious fake ads. Nuclear option.
